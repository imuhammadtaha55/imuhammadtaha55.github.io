ASSOCIATE DEGREE IN COMPUTER SCIENCES:

A two-year degree program in computer science is perfect for those who want to quickly start working, those planning to pursue a four-year degree, or those figuring out their career goals.
The main goals of this program are to teach participants the theories and math behind computing and the scientific principles of information technology. It's a great way to build a strong foundation for a career in the tech industry.
